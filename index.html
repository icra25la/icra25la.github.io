About this Satellite event:

ICRA 2025 LA@Chile is a satellite event of the ICRA conference. Its primary objective is to provide a remote participation opportunity for researchers and students from Latin America, in addition to local access to keynote talks. Many IEEE RAS members are unable to attend ICRA events due to financial constraints or visa restrictions. This satellite event aims to address these challenges.

We particularly encourage authors to share their in-progress works in the form of extended abstracts and short papers.

Travel grants applications: March 20th - April 22nd, 2025
Travel grants notifications: April 25th, 2025
Poster acceptance notifications: April 25th, 2025
